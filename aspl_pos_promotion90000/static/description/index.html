Odoo 14 POS Promotion (Community)

Allows user to get various Promotional Offers on products.

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo community edition but not tested in Enterprise version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.
You can't set multiple promotion rule on same product.

Features

  • Get Promotional Offers on Products
  • Define Brands on Products.
  • Buy X Item Get Y Item Free.
  • Buy X Item Get Discount on Y Item.
  • Discount(%) on Total Order Amount.
  • Percent Discount on Quantity.
  • Fixed Discount on Quantity.
  • Discount on Combination of Product.
  • Discount on Multiple Categories.

How to use

  • Click on "Pos Promotion" to set offers.
  • To get Promotional Offers, select any one of the "Promotion Type".

  • Buy X Get Y Free Promotion


  • Applied promotion can be seen as below.If user increased X product quantity then Y product quantity should be increased.
    For Example : X product quantity = 6 then Y product quantity = 3


  • Buy X Get Discount on Y Promotion


  • Applied promotion can be seen as below.


  • Discount(%) on Total Amount Promotion


  • Promotion applied if order total is equal to or grater than to based on setting total invoice amount.


  • Percent Discount on Quantity Promotion




  • Fixed Discount on Quantity Promotion




  • Discount on Combination Products Promotion




  • Discount on Combination Products Promotion




  • Applied promotions can also be seen on the Receipt Screen.

  • To define Brands for products click on "Configuration > Product > Product Brands".
